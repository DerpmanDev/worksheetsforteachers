This privacy and cookies policy outlines the types of personal information received and collected by Math-Drills and how it is used and discloses information about cookies that may be set on your computer by using the website. It was last updated on 2023-07-14.

Math-Drills Privacy Policy

Math-Drills.com keeps track of its visitors using server access logs. The information inside the server access logs includes Internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks. We use this information for security purposes, to analyze trends, administer the site, track users' movements around the site, and to gather demographic information. These logs are deleted monthly and only statistical information is retained.

Math-Drills.com collects email addresses and names through a double-opt-in process. You do not need to supply your email address to access our website, only if you wish to receive email news and updates from us. An unsubscribe link is available in every email that we send.

There are no social media widgets on Math-Drills therefore you will not be tracked by them while you use the website.

Math-Drills eliminates the bloat that you might find on other websites. We try very hard to have an extremely fast loading website. This means we don't force you to load any unnecessary code. This also means that we have fewer privacy implications since we rely on our own website code.

Math-Drills uses Google Adsense to serve ads to visitors. Google Adsense may track you using cookies (see Cookie Policy below) or other technology to serve you personalized ads based on your visits to Math-Drills or other sites on the Internet.

Math-Drills uses Google Analytics 4 to collect statistical information about how visitors use the website. This data is stored and processed in an anonymized way.

To learn more about the use of Google Services on Math-Drills, please see How Google Uses Information From Sites Or Apps That Use Our Services

Math-Drills Cookie Policy

To find and/or delete any cookies set by Math-Drills.com, search for cookies from "math-drills.com" using the view cookies functionality in your browser. Math-Drills uses cookies to store your consent preferences, if applicable, to set cookies starting with _ga for anonymous statistics collection via Google Analytics, and various other cookies used by Google Adsense and its Vendors.

If you access Math-Drills from a jurisdiction where consent is required

Math-Drills asks for your consent to use your personal data:

1. for personalised ads and content, ad and content measurement, audience insights, and product development; and

2. to store and/or access information on a device.

You have two options: Consent or Manage Options. Consent allows you to proceed and helps support the work of Math-Drills by allowing it to generate income from your use of the website and to gain insights on how the website is used. Managing options allows you to customize how Google and its vendors use your personal data.

Your consent to allow Google and its vendors to use your personal data will not override your browser settings. For example, if you block all cookies in your browser, you will not get any cookies as a result of using Math-Drills even if you have consented on the website.

If you access Math-Drills from anywhere else

You can customize your ads experience via Google's My Ad Center.
